About Us

TN Carpentry & Building

Qualified, Insured & Highly Experienced

TN Carpentry & Building is owned and run by Tom Naisbit.

Based in Belfast, we specialise in loft conversions, extensions and renovations.

For over 15 years, with the help of a trusted team of skilled tradespeople, we have been delivering excellent results on high-end domestic renovations and extensions.

We handle all aspects of the planning, design, project management, and build phases.
You will be consulted at all critical stages to to ensure you get your dream project is made a reality.
